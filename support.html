Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play WordBloom?

Swipe across the connected letters on the grid to form the hidden words. Words can run straight or bend in an L-shape. Find all the words to complete the level and unlock the next one.

Is the game free? Are there in-app purchases?

WordBloom is completely free to play. There are no in-app purchases and no subscriptions. The game is supported by ads only.

How do I remove ads?

The game is ad-supported and free. Watching the occasional ad keeps the game free for everyone. You can play offline (in airplane mode) if you prefer not to see ads.

How do I restore my progress on a new device?

Your level progress is stored locally on your device. If you switch devices and need help, email us and we'll do our best to assist.

I found a wrong word or the game crashed.

Please email us with your device model, iOS version, and a short description of what happened. Screenshots help a lot. We fix reported issues quickly.

Which languages are supported?

WordBloom includes word sets for multiple languages. More words and languages are added in updates.
