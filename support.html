Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play Word Bloom?

Swipe across the connected letters on the grid to form the hidden words. Words can be straight or bend in an L-shape. Find all the words to complete the level.

Is the game free?

Yes, Word Bloom is completely free to play. The game is supported by ads.

How do I restore my progress on a new device?

Your level progress is stored on your device. For help moving progress, contact us.

The game crashes or a word looks wrong.

Please email us with your device model and what happened. Screenshots help a lot. We fix reported issues quickly.
